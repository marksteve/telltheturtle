Legends speak of a certain turtle
that listens to people's tales.
Fact or fiction, it matters not -
as long as it's not stale.

Once you tell the turtle your story,
he will deliver it to another lost soul.
Somewhere out there you never know
who will take the reader's role.

Don't forget to check your mail,
for as you give, you shall receive
Though beware this creature's slowness
It may still come next next eve'
